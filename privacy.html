Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how CreatorFlow Studio handles information when creators use the application to generate, review, and publish short-form videos to connected creator accounts.

Information we process

CreatorFlow Studio may process content and account-related information needed to provide the service, including video topics, generated scripts, video files, captions, hashtags, publishing preferences, connected account authorization tokens, and basic account information returned by connected platforms.

How we use information

We use this information to generate video drafts, prepare publishing metadata, show review states to the creator, publish approved videos to connected accounts, maintain application logs, troubleshoot errors, and comply with platform API requirements.

Platform authorization

When a creator connects a platform account, authorization is handled through that platform's official OAuth or authorization flow. CreatorFlow Studio uses the granted permissions only for the features the creator requests, such as publishing an approved video.

Publishing control

Videos are not published automatically without creator approval. The creator reviews the final video and publishing metadata before the app sends the video to a connected platform.

Data sharing

We share information with connected platforms only as needed to perform requested actions, such as uploading an approved video and its caption. We do not sell personal information.

Data retention

Generated assets, logs, and publishing metadata may be retained for workflow continuity, review, troubleshooting, and audit purposes. Authorization tokens may be stored securely so the creator does not need to reconnect the same account for every upload.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect stored configuration, tokens, generated media, and workflow data. No system can guarantee absolute security.
