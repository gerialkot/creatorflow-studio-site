CreatorFlow Studio

CreatorFlow Studio is a workflow tool for generating, reviewing, and publishing short-form educational videos to connected creator accounts.

What the app does

The app helps creators prepare short-form video content by organizing topic research, script generation, video assembly, review, metadata generation, and creator-approved publishing.

Publishing only happens after the creator explicitly approves the final video and publishing metadata. Connected platform accounts are authorized by the account owner through the platform's official authorization flow.

Platform integrations

CreatorFlow Studio may connect to supported social platforms, including TikTok, to publish approved videos to the creator's own account according to the platform's API permissions and user consent.
